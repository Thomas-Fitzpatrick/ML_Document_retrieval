Introduction to Computer Science IIntroduction to computer science, the discipline of computing, and the programming language C++. Topics include elementary C++, computer algorithms, loops and flow of control, procedural abstraction, functions, streams, arrays, strings, objects and classes, polymorphism and operator overloading, professional societies, professionalism and ethics, pointers, dynamic memory, and dynamic arrays.
